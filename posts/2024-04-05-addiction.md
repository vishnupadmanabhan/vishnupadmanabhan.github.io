Comfort is the worst addiction. It can destroy you.

Comfort prevents growth. It keeps you stuck in one place. No good story ever started with -

>"He was sitting on his couch in comfort"

In every good story, someone has done something difficult.

Doing hard things brings opportunities. Doing uncomfortable things helps you grow.

Make more stories. Get uncomfortable with comfort. Get comfortable with discomfort.
