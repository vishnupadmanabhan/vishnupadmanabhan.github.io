title: "Expiration of dreams"
date: 2024-07-13
description: "If dreams are not acted on, they expire"

---


In my early 20s, I moved to a new city to find good employment.  To find a career where I could be rich and do stuff I always wanted to do. The notes I had written on the obscure corners of the internet confirmed that I had many plans and dreams.

Most of them stayed there, obscure and never materialised. With time, I forgot that they even existed. The list of things I had planned looks gigantic against the ones I acted upon and brought fruit. Thinking back, I realise that the dreams have an expiry date. If we do not act on the dreams today, they stop to exist after a while. 

My biggest fear is that there will come a day when my limited time  and I look back at these and wish -
> I should've started.

This needs to change.
