My wife and I took this weekend to travel a bit. We wanted to spent time at a resort away from the city.

Before the decision was made, a tussle was going on in our minds about whether we should. But it was shortlived and we went ahead to book the place.

Sitting on a nice desk near a well-lit window in our little cottage, I can confirm it was a good decision. We all need to do things and not overthink before doing it.

> Overcome the desire to think yourself out of having fun.

I am going to indulge myself in taking the leap, more often.
