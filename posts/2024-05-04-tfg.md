Sometimes, my wife and I reflect on things we are grateful for. Things that we thought were like a distant dream have become real now.

As we do this exercise, we are conscious of things we take for granted. If you have some time think about the following too. 

- Your Family
- Your Time
- Your Work & Income
- The Roof over your head
- The Support systems, the people who work to make your life easier
- People who came before us and did things for us
- Yourself

Think of each of the above and be grateful. If nothing, let that be the good deed for the day.
