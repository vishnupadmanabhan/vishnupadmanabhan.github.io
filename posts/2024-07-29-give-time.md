Last weekend, we went on a quick getaway to a resort in a village nearby with some friends. The agenda was simple, just chill and relax. After spending the evening swimming and playing around in the pool, we gathered to play group games.

One of the games suggested didn't excite me and I was not in favour of playing it. But we went ahead anyway. After about four rounds of the game, it started to interest me. I understood it better and started devising my own strategies to win the game and I did win a few of them. By the end of the day, we were all into it and had a great time!

The lesson I learnt is, to give time to settle in before giving up on something. Give it a few rounds, it may be the thing you want.
