---
title: "Music is Time Machine"
date: 2024-11-19
description: "Music is the best time machine"
---


Music has always been a part of my life. It’s rare for a day to pass without a track playing in the background or me actively listening to something.

As a child, the radio was always on in our home. Later, cassettes introduced by cousins and friends became my go to. Eventually, MP3 players and CDs took over, changing how I listened but not what music meant to me.

Certain songs take me back to specific moments in my life, simpler, quieter times. Nostalgia hits not because I miss the music itself but because I miss how life felt when I first heard it.

Where does your playlist take you?
