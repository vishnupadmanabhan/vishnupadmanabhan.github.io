It has become hard to decide what direction life needs to take. For younger adults, their decision is made by social media and the icons they follow online. Traditional values of life do not hold good for them anymore and life is dictated by a 4x5 image posted on the social media sites.

Along with this, we have lost our right to make decisions for ourselves. Decisions are made for us. What to do, how to do, where to do and when to do. These questions are answered for us instead of by us.

Life isn't what you see in a frame inside your phone. Everything outside the frame matters. 

Don't chase the Instagram life.
